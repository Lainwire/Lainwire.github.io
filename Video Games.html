Hello again, same as cartoons anime and manga except this will be a list a lot sooner
since I don't play that many video games. Still have favourites though.

Celeste Such an engaging platformer that's really well designed. It doesn't feel
as fustrating as other games despite it's difficulty because it has the
quick respawning after death. Madelines controls extremely well, there
is no slipperly ness and that control makes it so precise to play. The
story is surpisingly deep and is quite emotionally resenonant, and the
music is goated.
Terraria A game I grew up through many versions, just such a great sandbox game.
It's progession is amazing and seeing your power increase throughout the
entire game is so rewarding to watch. It's surpringsly tough too on the
master mode difficulty even for someone whose played this game so much
(wouldn't reccomend it for a starter playthrough but it's the way to play)
The bosses are fun, the weapons are great and fun to use, though kinda
wish there was some leveling system so I could use obselete weapons into
the endgame, but that would break the progression so might not be for
the best. It's a charming, extremely fun game that you should play at
least once, it's cheap af as well.
Portal 2 Another classic valve game, just an amazing puzzle platformer that expands
so greatly from the first. I kinda forget how short the original is, it feels
like a proof of concept with it's length, it's still an enjoyable time but
portal 2 is what is needed. The puzzles are so clever and I love how they
evolve over the course of the game and how dynamic it all is, valve did so
much with so little here. The story is so engaging and uncovering the lore
behind aperture science through the old labs is amazing, and the characters
are so interesting. The game as a whole is so funny, the comedy works so well
for me too. Plus it's got cave johnson, you can't go wrong.
